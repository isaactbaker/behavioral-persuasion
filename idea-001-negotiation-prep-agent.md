# idea-001: Negotiation Prep Agent

## Problem
Sales teams and procurement departments leave millions on the table because negotiators have no systematic prep, no safe practice environment, and no post-deal analysis. Current solutions: $3,000/seat one-shot workshops with zero follow-up, or generic courses with no practice.

## Target Customer
VP of Sales at B2B SaaS companies ($10M-$500M ARR) with 10+ person sales teams closing $50K+ ACV deals. Also: procurement directors at mid-market to enterprise companies.

## Solution Concept
An AI agent (Slack/Teams-integrated) that:
1. Preps for negotiations by analyzing the counterparty and generating persuasion strategy before calls
2. Runs roleplay scenarios with dynamic buyer personas (procurement, technical, executive)
3. Provides real-time persuasion tactics during calls (whisper mode)
4. Debriefs after calls tracking what persuasion principles worked
5. Builds a per-rep persuasion profile that improves over time

## Validation Hypothesis
"If I show a VP of Sales a landing page describing this at $299/seat/mo, 5% of visitors will join a paid waitlist or buy early access."

## Price Point
$299/seat/mo — positioned between cheap online courses ($500/seat) and expensive workshops ($3,000/seat)

## Competition
- Karrass ($1,500-3,000/seat workshops) — no tech, no follow-up
- Scotwork ($2,000-5,000) — consulting only
- Black Swan Group ($2,000-4,000) — training only
- Pactum AI (enterprise, $100K+) — replaces negotiators, doesn't train
- Gong ($700-2,000/seat/yr) — tells WHAT was said, not HOW to improve

## Risk Level
Medium — SMB sales teams may have smaller budgets, but mid-market and enterprise VPs have training budget and the pain is acute (lost deals). Clearest ROI of all three products.

## Key Differentiator
Persuasion science backed by PhD-level expertise, not generic AI advice. Cialdini, reframing, BATNA analysis, cognitive biases — applied algorithmically, not as workshop theory.
