# idea-003: Executive Communication Coach

## Problem
Executives and VPs are judged on communication every day — meetings, email, presentations, Slack — but nobody gives them honest feedback. Their direct reports won't tell them they come across as cold, dismissive, or unclear. Expensive executive coaches ($15K-50K/yr) only work with the C-suite. Everyone else is flying blind.

## Target Customer
VPs and Directors at mid-market to enterprise companies ($100M+ revenue). Particularly: engineering VPs who need to build rapport with non-technical stakeholders, new managers transitioning from IC roles, and senior leaders preparing for board-level presence.

## Solution Concept
A private AI agent accessible via Slack/Teams/WhatsApp/Telegram that:
1. Analyzes meeting transcripts for warmth vs authority balance, empathy markers, and social intelligence cues
2. Reviews email/chat drafts and suggests alternative framings based on charisma science
3. Roleplays difficult conversations (performance reviews, budget pitches, tough feedback)
4. Tracks weekly improvement on specific social dimensions (active listening, perspective-taking, perceived trustworthiness)
5. Never judges — private, personalized, compounding feedback loop

## Validation Hypothesis
"If I show a VP-level leader a landing page at $99/mo individual or $249/seat/mo team that promises honest, science-backed communication feedback, 4% will join a paid waitlist."

## Price Point
$99/mo (individual), $249/seat/mo (team of 3-10 execs), enterprise custom

## Competition
- BetterUp ($300-600/mo) — human coaches, expensive
- Poised ($15-20/user/mo) — shallow metrics (filler words only)
- Quantified Communications ($1,500-3,000/leader) — diagnostics only, no coaching loop
- Yoodli ($16-30/user/mo) — speech-focused, no written comms
- DDI/Korn Ferry ($15K-40K/yr per leader) — elite pricing

None offer a continuous, private, multi-channel communication coach that builds a deepening model of the user.

## Risk Level
Medium — enterprise sales cycles are long, but $99/mo individual plan self-serves. Risk is low for the individual plan test.

## Key Differentiator
PhD-level charisma science applied algorithmically across ALL communication channels (meetings, email, chat, presentations). Warmth/authority framework, empathy detection, perspective-taking analysis — based on actual social psychology research, not generic "communication tips."
