The 6 dimensions

What we measure

Warmth

Friendliness, approachability, genuine interest. The strongest predictor of perceived charisma.

Authority

Confidence, competence signaling, decisiveness. Traded off against warmth — we show you the calibration.

Empathy

Cognitive empathy — perspective-taking and validation. Not sympathy. The dimension that builds trust.

Active Listening

Responding to what was actually said vs. what you expected. Reflecting, building on others' points.

Clarity

Structure, conciseness, signal-to-noise ratio. How quickly do people understand you?

Trust Signals

Perceived honesty, reliability, congruence between words and intent. The hardest dimension to improve.
